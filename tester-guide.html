SongLab · MVP tester guide

Help me test SongLab.

Record-crafting software that teaches you how the pros build records — then hands you the technique to use on your own song. It coaches; you create.

Open SongLab songlab-mvp.onrender.com

Takes ~5–10 minutes · no account · you can't break anything that matters, so poke around hard. First load can take ~30–50s to wake up (free server having a nap).

Before you start

So you know what's normal

  • Best on a laptop/desktop browser for now — mobile works but is rougher.
  • First load is slow (cold start). Quick after that.
  • The "AI Analyst" is intentionally basic right now — that part's coming.
  • No login; your work lives in your browser only.
The walkthrough
1
1 min

Set your lane

Left sidebar → Mode: Songwriter · Level: Starter · Genre: pick any (e.g. R&B).

ExpectA short, calm menu (~4 rooms), and the whole app re-tints to your genre.

Tell me: Did "Starter" feel welcoming or too bare? Was Mode-vs-Level clear?

2
3 min · the core idea

Steal a move

Pro Playbook → pick a producer → scroll to "Steal this move" → read steps 1–2 → in step 3, write your own version in the box → Add to my bag → open Reference Vault.

ExpectIt never writes the lyric for you — your own words get saved.

Tell me: Did it feel like it was teaching you, not doing it for you? Was the "now you try" prompt doable?

3
1 min

Watch it grow

Change Level: Starter → Standard → Deep.

ExpectMore rooms unlock in the menu as the level rises.

Tell me: Does "the app grows with your skill" make sense — useful or gimmicky?

4
2 min

Diagnose a song

Song Doctor → make up a title / concept / hook, set the sliders → Run Diagnosis.

ExpectA score + a specific "first fix" + weak / watch areas.

Tell me: Was the feedback specific enough to act on? Did the verdict feel fair?

5
2 min

Explore the craft library

Browse Case Studies, Session Greats, and the Glossary.

Tell me: Did anything teach you something new? Anything you wanted explained that wasn't?

6
optional

Audio Lab

Open Audio Lab → Demo Groove or upload a beat → try the loop + markers.

Tell me: Did playback / looping work? Anything confusing?
